ROLE

Air Wing NCC plays a very important role in the school and college days. The NCC is an organization which gives admission to the selected cadets from schools, colleges all over India. The cadets given strict military training by performing some activities that includes physical activities, parade, study, flying, social activities, running and many more. NCC cadets will given preference over normal candidates during selection in air force field. NCC which helps cadets to develop leadership, punctuality, comradeship, sportsmanship, courage, amd many qualities to make them a patriotic citizen. To make them strong, disciplined so that they will be available for their nation.
