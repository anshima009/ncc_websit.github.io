CAMP

Camp training is the practical manifestation of institutional training. The aim of camp training is to introduce cadets in developing comradeship, work team, leadership, self-confidence, in the cadets. It is compulsory for the cadets to attend at least one camp for 'B' Certificate exam and compulsory to attend two camps for 'C' certificate exam.

YEP

YOUTH EXCHANGE PROGRAMME

Youth Exchange Programe in which country to country NCC selected cadet's participate. NCC has a vibrant YEP with 11 countries. More than 100 cadets proceed abroad on YEP annualy. They participate in NCC activities of the host country to create an increased awareness and appreciation of each others socio-economic and cultural realities. NCC cadets visit historical places in the respective country and the places they are famous for. NCC cadets participate in cultural activities.

RDC CAMP

REPUBLIC DAY CAMP

This camp is held at Garrisson Parade Ground, Delhi Cantt every year from 01 Jan to 29 Jan. 2070 selected NCC cadets from all the States and Union Territories attend the camp. NCC cadets visit famous places of Delhi and learn many more things.

AIVSC CAMP

ALL INDIA VAYU SAINIK CAMP

This is essentially a Centrally Organized Training Camp for eligible Air Wing SD and SW cadets organized and conducted by Directorate Gen AIVSC conducted at Jodhpur.

CATC CAMP

COMBINED ANNUAL TRAINING CAMP

This is a combined camp of boys and girls in which various kinds of activities such as Games, Cultural activities, Educational activities, NCC activities - Drill, Shooting etc., and many more activities are organized.
